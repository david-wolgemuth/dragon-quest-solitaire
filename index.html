Game Rules & Card Reference

Objective

Defeat the Dragon Queen and exit the dungeon to win!

Setup

  • Health: Start with 5 health (Hearts A-5)
  • Gems: 10 gems available (Diamonds A-10)
  • Inventory: 7 items shuffled (Hearts J-K, Diamonds J-K, Red Joker)
  • Fate Deck: 5 cards (Hearts 6-10) for combat
  • Dungeon: 27 cards (all Spades, all Clubs, Black Joker)

How to Play

Start with one face-down card in the center. Each turn:

  1. Place a card: Add an adjacent card from the dungeon deck (next to face-down cards)
  2. Resolve a card: Click a face-down card to flip and resolve it

Combat

When fighting enemies, draw from the Fate Deck:

  • Success: Meet or exceed required value = no damage
  • Critical (10): Bonus reward!
  • Failure: Take damage from the enemy

Winning & Losing

  • Win: Defeat Dragon Queen (Queen of Spades), then reach an Exit (Ace)
  • Lose: Health drops to 0
